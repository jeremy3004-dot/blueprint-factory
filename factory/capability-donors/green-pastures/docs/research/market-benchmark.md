# Himalayan Passage Research Notes

## What top trekking operators surface well

### Intrepid Travel
- Route pages prominently show duration, group size, physical rating, style tiers, start/end points, pricing, dates, review count, and “Why you’ll love this trip.”
- Everest and Annapurna pages also surface alternate itinerary logic when Lukla weather disrupts flights, which is a strong trust feature to copy.
- Source:
  - https://www.intrepidtravel.com/us/nepal/everest-base-camp-trek-144944
  - https://intrepidtravel.com/us/nepal/annapurna-circuit-trek-167247

### World Expeditions
- Strongest operational detail layer: accommodation mix, meals count, difficulty grading, carbon footprint, map, guide section, responsible travel, supplements, and extra trip options.
- Their pages feel useful because they quantify logistics instead of hiding them.
- Source:
  - https://assets.worldexpeditions.com/Nepal/Trekking-Walking/Everest-Three-Passes-Trek
  - https://assets.worldexpeditions.com/Nepal/Trekking-Walking/Everest-Trek-in-Comfort

### G Adventures
- Excellent FAQ depth: visa requirements, insurance expectations, tipping, spending money, laundry, safety, transport, age rules, and joining instructions.
- Also shows “Need a flight? We can help” and premium collection positioning that ties style and service level to the product.
- Source:
  - https://www.gadventures.com/trips/annapurna-circuit-trek/ANTA/details/
  - https://www.gadventures.com/trips/nepal-annapurna-hiking-rafting-and-chitwan-national-park/ANKG/

## What we copied into the app
- Clear route intelligence: duration, altitude, region, difficulty, permits, access logic, and premium bundle ideas.
- Trust-building logistics: guide profiles, hybrid heli and jeep packages, and practical prep notes.
- Utility over vague marketing: route explorer, booking builder, and AI concierge instead of brochure-only sections.
- Pre-travel guidance: visa, insurance, permits, cash, and connectivity checklist exposed early.

## Nepal prep sources used for framing
- The Nepal Embassy notice references the online visa portal (`nepaliport.immigration.gov.np`) for visa-on-arrival preparation:

## Product takeaway
- Premium travel sites win by pairing emotion with logistics.
- The best ones do not just sell mountains. They reduce uncertainty.
- This build leans hard into that.
