# Dorje's Aman-Led Website Design

## Goal

Build a high-production, three-page Dorje's Resort & Spa website whose structure, pacing and interaction model are visibly descended from Aman's homepage while every shipped fact and asset belongs to Dorje's.

## Architecture

The site stays self-contained under `sites/dorjes/app`. A shared client shell owns the sticky header, accessible menu, footer and reveal behavior. The homepage and two commercial inner pages consume typed content objects and local client-owned assets; no CMS, auth, database or new booking engine is introduced.

## Experience

The visual concept is “Stillness Above the Lake.” Aman contributes the contained cinematic hero, asymmetric editorial pair, clipped horizontal card rail, quiet serif/sans hierarchy, generous breathing space and restrained conversion close. Dorje's contributes the resort film, lake and room photography, warm ivory/olive palette, founder story and all business facts.

The signature moment is “The Lake Aperture”: Dorje's own film settles inside a precise parchment frame while the mark and promise rise into place, then hands off to a layered editorial journey through rooms, lake, food and wellness. The experience becomes static and fully visible under reduced motion.

## Pages

- Home: brand introduction, stay/wellness pair, experience rail, resort stories, sustainability/founder band and booking close.
- Stay: all four real room categories and sourced descriptions, with rates deferred to live availability because the current pages conflict.
- Dining: local produce, lake-bar dining, Moondance, Dorje's Bar & Grill and The Hearth private dining.

## Quality and safety

All Aman media and copy remain reference-only. All production media is downloaded from Dorje's current website and logged. The menu is keyboard operable, focus styles are visible, imagery has meaningful alt text, motion honors `prefers-reduced-motion`, and external booking/contact links are labeled honestly.
