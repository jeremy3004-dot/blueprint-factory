# BigMart Reference-First Topology

## Primary Donor: Natoora

Captured:
- `natoora-desktop.png`
- `natoora-mobile.png`

Moves to borrow:
- Minimal white nav over a food-first page.
- Full-width produce image as the first brand signal.
- Centered selector modal as the functional first interaction.
- Editorial image section with headline overlay.
- Acid campaign band that breaks the page rhythm.
- Seasonal/product cards with image, title, and dense editorial copy.
- App CTA band with illustration/line-art feel.
- Manifesto close about food system and mission.

## BigMart Translation

Use Natoora's structure as the base, not its brand. BigMart should open with real produce imagery and a selector card that makes the public promise "Your Neighbor" functional. Use official BigMart assets, app screenshots, orange/blue identity, and conservative public claims.
