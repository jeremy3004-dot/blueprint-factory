How it Works
1. Shop on your schedule. Subscribe to your favorite staples or place an à la carte order.
2. Our farmers harvest your food. By ordering only what we need, we keep our waste under 1%!
3. On the day of your delivery, we pack up your box and bring it to your door.

What Sets Us Apart
Grown Today, Delivered Tomorrow

We are the fastest solution from farm to table. Our rotating selection of seasonal produce is chock-full of precious nutrients that won’t be depleted by weeks of travel and storage.

Not Your Average Grocery Delivery

Shop our full range (800+) of sustainable groceries sourced with care and expertise. Cutting out the middleman gives you a better value while the farmers and makers earn more, too.

Our Name is Our Mission
Farm to People is on a mission to radically transform the food system and the way people get food to their table. We believe everyone should have access to the freshest, high-quality produce, seafood, meats, and more direct from growers and producers they can trust — because they know them. We’re working to make that easy and affordable.
