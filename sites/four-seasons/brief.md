# Brief: four-seasons

Status: draft

## What This Site Is

A reference-first homepage clone of Four Seasons: cinematic hospitality, booking-first utility, editorial property discovery, and premium global travel storytelling.

## Audience

Luxury travelers comparing resort stays, loyalty guests returning to Four Seasons, couples and families planning seasonal trips, and brand reviewers checking whether the clone captures the donor's polish.

## Inputs Provided

- Donor: `https://www.fourseasons.com/`
- Donor evidence: desktop screenshot and parallel-agent desktop/mobile behavior notes.
- Required workflow: clone the donor's structure, rhythm, motion, and interaction quality before any brand translation.

## Source Notes

The first build should match Four Seasons' media-led structure: full-bleed hero, transparent sticky nav, booking/search utility, category rail, promo image band, featured property carousel, editorial cards, story CTA, and black footer.

## Required Pages Or Sections

- Transparent sticky header over a full-bleed destination hero.
- Booking/search controls with destination, dates, guests, and CTA.
- Dark promotional strip and image-led Mexico-style campaign band.
- Featured Properties tabs and carousel.
- Explore More three-card editorial grid.
- Centered story CTA.
- Black footer with compact luxury navigation.
- Mobile menu overlay, booking bottom sheet, search suggestions, and scroll-snap property cards.

## Deploy Expectation

Local preview and QA only. Ask before production deploy.

## Ready Criteria

- Donor desktop and mobile screenshots exist.
- Topology notes exist.
- `art-direction.md` names one signature moment.
- Desktop hero, sticky nav, booking flow, carousel, menu overlay, and mobile sheet are verified.
- Desktop and mobile screenshots are captured and visually reviewed against the donor.
