## Donor Structure

1. Thin dark promo strip.
2. Transparent header over a full-bleed yacht/destination hero.
3. Hero headline with one restrained CTA.
4. Bottom category rail with icon-like labels.
5. Dark campaign image band for the Mexico collection.
6. Featured Properties tabs and carousel.
7. Explore More three-card editorial grid on white.
8. Centered story CTA on black.
9. Black footer with compact global hospitality links.

## Desktop Interaction Model

- Hero is media-led and full-bleed.
- Nav begins transparent and becomes sticky/solid as the page scrolls.
- Booking/search is direct and visible.
- Featured Properties uses tabbed switching plus carousel browsing.

## Mobile Interaction Model

- Compact header with menu and check-rates actions.
- Full-screen menu overlay.
- Booking bottom sheet.
- Search suggestions.
- Scroll-snap property cards.

## Moves To Borrow

- Full-bleed destination media as the first emotional signal.
- Transparent-to-sticky luxury nav.
- Booking utility placed early without feeling like a SaaS form.
- Dark promo strip as a rhythm break.
- Destination image band for seasonal campaign focus.
- Tabbed property carousel for browsing depth.
- Three-card editorial grid for lighter discovery.
- Black footer as the final premium anchor.

## Anti-Copy Rules

- Clone the structure, rhythm, and interaction quality.
- Do not ship protected Four Seasons media, logos, or copy without approval.
- Replace donor assets during translation or production hardening.
