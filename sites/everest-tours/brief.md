# Brief: Everest Tours

Status: complete

## What This Site Is

Everest Tours is a fictional Kathmandu-based premium trekking and cultural tour company for this test job. The site should feel like a calm, expert operator for foreign travelers who want small-group Nepal journeys planned by people who know the mountain.

## Audience

English-speaking travelers from the US, UK, EU, and Australia who are considering Nepal for Everest Base Camp, Annapurna, Mustang, or culture-led Kathmandu Valley journeys. They likely care about safety, guide quality, logistics, altitude pacing, and a travel experience that feels personal without being overhyped.
