OUR RESORT IN POKHARA

### Where Comfortmeets Serenity
Dorje’s Resort & Spa is an eco-luxury boutique resort in Sedi Hills, Pokhara, overlooking Phewa Lake. With 18 exclusive rooms and suites, fine dining, spa and wellness facilities, swimming pool, outdoor jacuzzi, private dining experiences, chauffeur-driven transfers, curated sightseeing, and peaceful natural surroundings, Dorje’s offers a refined escape for couples, families, wellness travelers, and private retreats.

## A Dreamer and A Do-Er!
Dorje is well-travelled, a people’s person, who speaks 7 languages. Compassionate by nature, he practises meditation, and believes in overall well-being. He really enjoys his work and finds satisfaction in bringing a smile to those he meets and his clients. An avid tennis player, he is also the President of the Kaski Tennis Association. Dorje’s Resort & Spa is his dream project that has now come to life after years of meticulous planning keeping the local community at the heart of all his decisions.
