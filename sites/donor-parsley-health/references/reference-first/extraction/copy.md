### What makes Parsey Health different from my PCP or other telehealth providers?
- [button] What makes Parsey Health different from my PCP or other telehealth providers?
What makes Parsey Health different from my PCP or other telehealth providers?
The traditional healthcare system is built to treat acute symptoms, like illnesses, injuries, and emergencies. Parsley practices functional medicine, which is built to understand what’s going on at the root of the symptom. We’re concerned with helping you feel better in the present and build the foundation for sustained health over time.
We combine advanced lab testing with longer visits and ongoing support to uncover the underlying drivers of complex conditions and multi-system symptoms. Then, using evidence-based protocols, your provider creates a custom care plan that is designed to resolve symptoms, optimize your health, and extend your health span. Your dedicated care team considers the full picture of your health and how all 9 body systems interact to impact how you feel.
It’s a more complete, comprehensive, and connected approach that results in real progress you can measure and feel in your day-to-day life.
