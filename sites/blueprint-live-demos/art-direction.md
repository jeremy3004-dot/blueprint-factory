# Art Direction: Blueprint Factory — Live Demos

## Primary Donor

No single visual donor. The page is an original Blueprint Factory editorial frame designed to stay out of the way of ten very different live projects and client demos.

## Secondary References

- Contact-sheet portfolios from design studios.
- Oversized horizontal project rails from editorial product launches.
- Restrained museum placards for metadata and disclaimers.

## Signature Moment

**The Oversized Demo Rail** — a full-width horizontal carousel where each card is large enough to read as a finished website contact print, not a thumbnail grid tile. Visitors feel the breadth of the collection through scroll, swipe, and keyboard movement before opening any demo full screen.

## Moves Being Borrowed

- Editorial headline + stat row for studio positioning.
- Native scroll snapping instead of JavaScript auto-advance.
- Duplicated registry rendering for carousel and static index.
- Terracotta Live Demo badge as the only loud accent on an otherwise paper-and-ink frame.

## Exclusions

- No iframe embeds.
- No auto-advancing carousel.
- No CMS, auth, analytics, or lead capture in v1.
