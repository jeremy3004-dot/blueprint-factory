# Art Direction: one-and-only-resorts

Date: 2026-06-30
Status: draft
Owner sign-off: pending

## 1. What this site is

A One&Only Resorts homepage clone for Blueprint Factory, focused on cinematic arrival, resort discovery, editorial calm, and booking confidence.

## 2. The visual world

Luxury editorial resort minimalism: warm white space, real destination media, small uppercase labels, delicate serif headlines, and quiet navigation.

## 3. The signature moment (required)

Resort Aperture Reveal: on load, the white page opens into the living resort hero video while the One&Only headline lifts in two calm lines over the water.

## 4. Motion language

- Pace: slow, gliding, and composed.
- Easing feel: polished editorial ease, never springy or playful.
- Scroll feel: native, with once-only reveals that feel like pages in a magazine.
- Restraint: motion belongs to the hero, gallery, sticky/menu/booking overlays, and small hover states only.

## 5. Typography

- Display: Cormorant Garamond, echoing the donor's high-contrast hospitality serif.
- Body/UI: DM Sans for quiet clarity in nav, labels, forms, and captions.
- Character move: tiny uppercase metadata above airy serif headlines.

## 6. Color world

- Base: white, soft stone, charcoal, and photography-led color.
- Accents: only subtle linework and warm paper tones.
- Overall feel: bright, sparse, and image-led.
- Rule: no black/gold luxury cliche and no saturated gradients.

## 7. Layout system

- Grid feel: generous and editorial, with centered hero copy, inset destination panel, split feature blocks, and horizontal galleries.
- Sections alternate full media, split editorial, centered quiet text, card carousel, and footer utility.
- First-screen plan: a fixed white header, living resort media, centered brand headline, and a hint of the next editorial destination panel after scroll.

## 8. Reference comparanda

- Primary donor: `https://www.oneandonlyresorts.com/?utm_source=chatgpt.com`. Clone-derived structure: centered-logo header, full-screen video hero, inset global-destinations panel, split stays feature, centered experience intro, folding gallery, seasonal story cards, private homes block, quiet story/offers sections, benefits row, and calm footer.
- Donor screenshots captured: desktop `sites/one-and-only-resorts/references/reference-first/oneandonly-desktop.png`; mobile `sites/one-and-only-resorts/references/reference-first/oneandonly-mobile.png`.
- Donor topology notes: `sites/one-and-only-resorts/references/reference-first/topology.md`.

- Reference 1: One&Only live site motion. Steal: living hero media, slow editorial reveals, and restrained gallery changes.
- Reference 2: Blueprint Factory Four Seasons clone. Steal: focus-safe overlays, reduced-motion guard, and typed Next/React implementation discipline.
- Reference 3: Blueprint Factory Beauty Pass rubric. Steal: donor screenshot comparison before taste judgment.

## 9. Anti-goals

- No generic luxury homepage with dark overlay, huge centered CTA, and three equal cards.
- No SaaS bento grids, icon rows, fake stats, logo strips, or marketing badge clutter.
- No overdone black/gold palette.
- No frantic parallax, cursor tricks, bouncing reveals, or decorative motion.
- No production use of protected donor media without replacement/licensing review.

## 10. Deploy and backend expectation

- Deploy profile expected: Vercel preview.
- Backend expected: none for this clone preview.

## 11. What must be true before this site is called ready

- Desktop and mobile screenshots resemble the donor's structure and sparse density.
- Hero video/fallback, folding gallery, footer accordions, menu overlay, and booking overlay are tested.
- Motion capture proves the Resort Aperture Reveal and calm scroll rhythm.
- Reduced-motion keeps content visible.
- Visual review records scores against the donor screenshots.
