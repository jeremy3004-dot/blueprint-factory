Hi, we’re WHOA, a boutique, women-powered adventure company, with big dreams. We believe traveling is about being changed, and being the change you want to see in the world. Our passion is designing sustainable adventures that help women reach new peaks, in the mountains we climb (hello Kilimanjaro!) and beyond. We believe travel, when done right, has a ripple effect that will transform you and the world!

STAND STRONG


We’re an inclusive community that knows we are stronger and unstoppable TOGETHER. We lift each other up & celebrate our differences. We do more than climb mountains—we move them!

DREAM BIG

A NOTE FROM WHOA CO-FOUNDERS


When we started WHOA, we were two young women looking for more out of life than the grind of New York City. We had no idea that our Kilimanjaro-sized dream would change our lives so much, or that it would take on a life of its own and evolve into the strong community of adventurous women it is today! A lot has changed since the beginning days, but so much has stayed true to our foundation. Our curiosity, passion and willingness to learn is still there and growing. We still operate from our hearts, and believe that there’s a bigger purpose and meaning in the adventures we take. We think you ended up here, reading this, for a reason. We’re honored and ready to be by your side on your journey, and to have you beside us on ours! Together, we’re unstoppable!
