# Brief: San Chon Korean Restaurant

Status: approved from the owner's supplied donor and source brief

## What This Site Is

A complete public restaurant website for San Chon Korean Food in Pokhara. It uses COTE Korean Steakhouse as the structural and motion donor, then replaces every donor identity surface with San Chon's verified Lakeside location, Korean menu cues, practical visit information, and original production imagery.

## Audience

- Pokhara residents looking for Korean barbecue and familiar Korean dishes.
- Lakeside visitors and trekkers who want a relaxed shared meal.
- Korean and international travelers looking for a calm, recognizable dining experience.

## Inputs Provided

- Primary visual donor: `https://www.cotekoreansteakhouse.com/`.
- Brand/content source: San Chon's Tripadvisor listing.
- Owner direction: complete the site without follow-up questions.

## Verified Source Notes

- Public name: SanChon Korean Food / Sanchon Korean Restaurant / `산촌다람쥐`.
- Location: Lakeside 6 / Street 16, Pokhara 33700, Nepal.
- Phone carried by Tripadvisor and the restaurant's public social mirror: `+977 982-4147894`.
- Public Instagram: `@san_chon_korean_restaurant`.
- Tripadvisor categories: barbecue, Asian, Korean; lunch and dinner; reservations, seating, and table service.
- Public listings commonly show daily noon–10 PM, but Tripadvisor itself does not publish hours. The site labels this schedule as online-listed and asks guests to confirm on Instagram.
- Exact prices, awards, chef biography, owner story, and a complete current menu are not claimed.

## Required Pages

1. `/` — donor-faithful home experience and primary reservation/visit funnel.
2. `/menu` — a carefully qualified taste of publicly documented dishes, without invented prices.
3. `/visit` — address, online-listed hours, phone, Instagram, directions, and dining notes.

## Deploy Expectation

Vercel preview only. No production deploy without explicit approval. Backend, CMS, auth, online ordering, newsletter storage, and database are not required.

- COTE desktop/mobile topology and section rhythm are visibly recognizable after translation.
- The Ember Seal hero reveal and sensory collage land in normal motion and collapse cleanly for reduced motion.
- All three routes build, capture, and pass links, console, accessibility, and responsive checks.
- No donor images, donor copy, Tripadvisor traveler photos, invented prices, or fabricated testimonials ship.
- All production imagery and source decisions are recorded in `asset-log.md` and `references/brand-source/source-notes.md`.
