# Inner Pages Specification

## Overview

- Targets: `/menu`, `/visit`
- Donor sources: COTE menu/location route families from harvested inventory
- Interaction model: static local content with anchor and external links

## Shared Shell

- Reuse announcement, sticky header, reservation rail, visit panel, and footer.
- Page hero is 62svh desktop / 56svh mobile with full-bleed original media, dark scrim, eyebrow, and oversized serif title.

## Menu

- Intro explicitly says the page is a taste of publicly documented favorites and availability changes.
- Sections: At the Grill; Rice, Noodles & Bowls; Small Plates & Sides.
- No prices and no claim of completeness.
- Dish names are limited to documented public sources.

## Visit

- Full operational card, CSS map, transport/location note, large-share-plate note, reservation handoff, and source qualifier for hours.
- Do not embed a third-party map or tracking script.

## Responsive

- Menu uses a 12-column editorial list desktop and single-column ruled list mobile.
- Visit uses asymmetric 5/7 grid desktop and one column mobile.
