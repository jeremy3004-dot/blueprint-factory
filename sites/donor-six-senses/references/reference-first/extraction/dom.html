WELLNESS PROGRAMS

Kamalaya’s innovative philosophy to wellness supports holistic healing from the inside out. Flexibility and fluidity are at the heart of our offerings, inviting you to listen to your intuition as you seek internal harmony. Every individual who walks through our doors has unique needs, and at Kamalaya, we provide the time, space, and support to help you define and refine your personal wellness journey.

We believe that all aspects of health are interconnected. Our programs are therefore enriched by the expertise of Naturopaths, Traditional Chinese Medicine practitioners, Yoga teachers, fitness trainers, massage therapists, Meditation and Life Enhancement Mentors, and a variety of other complementary health professionals.

FIND YOUR ACCOMMODATION

TRANQUIL HAVENS FOR YOUR TRANSFORMATION

At Kamalaya, we offer 76 thoughtfully designed rooms, suites, and villas, each harmoniously integrated into the natural landscape of granite boulders, lush forest canopies, and sweeping sea views. Many of our accommodations feature breathtaking views overlooking the verdant jungle or Koh Samui’s turquoise waters, nestled in the Gulf of Thailand.

Transcending a traditional spa retreat, Kamalaya is a sanctuary for the soul, where every detail – from the architecture to the cuisine – invites profound rest, rejuvenation, and a deep reconnection with your true self.

DIGITAL DETOX

We encourage you to abandon the regular use of digital devices to ensure that you and your fellow guests are able to disconnect from the outside world and fully immerse in a wellness program. We kindly request you to not use mobiles, laptops, tablets or other digital devices, except e-books, in public areas of tranquillity. All guests receive a complimentary journal to jot down any flashes of inspiration that manifest whilst relaxing on the beach, dining in our restaurants or strolling around our expansive tropical gardens.

Guests are more than welcome to use digital devices in the privacy of their own rooms or in the Library, where complimentary WiFi is available.
