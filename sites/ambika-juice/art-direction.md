# Art Direction: Ambika Juice Pokhara

Date: 2026-07-10
Status: approved
Owner sign-off: yes

## 1. What this site is

A premium, discovery-led digital flagship for a beloved Pokhara juice shop, pairing a complete drink catalogue with a clear path to visit the Rastra Bank Chowk location.

## 2. The visual world

Exact Onyx editorial commerce in the clone stage; in translation, tropical produce photographed with the gravity of luxury product design—warm, tactile, graphic, and unmistakably Pokhara.

## 3. The signature moment (required)

**The Living Menu Wall:** the donor's arched origin carousel becomes a richly photographed Ambika drink-category wall whose panels glide and recompose with the exact restrained Swiper timing, then open directly into category and drink pages.

## 4. Motion language

- Pace: slow and weighted in full-bleed media, crisp in navigation and filters.
- Easing: smooth deceleration without elastic overshoot.
- Scroll: native and precise; no heavy smoothing unless live donor evidence requires it.
- Restraint: editorial cream sections stay quiet; video, carousel, and mega-menu states carry the motion.
- Reduced motion: final states render immediately, autoplay stops, and every rail retains manual controls.

## 5. Typography

- Display: an open extended grotesk chosen to match Montserrat/Kapra width and uppercase authority.
- Body: a warm open geometric sans chosen to match Bajern's compact editorial texture.
- Character move: widely tracked uppercase headlines broken into deliberate short lines, with tiny labels and arrow links.

## 6. Color world

- Clone base: donor black `#000000`, warm paper `#fbfaf3`, white, and muted cream.
- Ambika base: warm fruit-cream, high-contrast ink, leafy green, mango gold, and guava pink.
- Overall feel: light editorial sections punctuated by cinematic dark/full-bleed fruit and preparation media.
- Rule: accents live in photography and campaign moments; interface chrome stays restrained and highly legible.

## 6b. Token translation (donor -> brand)

- Colors changed from donor -> brand after the measured clone: black/paper core remains for structure; campaign accents shift from coffee/collaboration colors to mango, guava, citrus, and leaf tones.
- Fonts changed from donor -> brand: proprietary Bajern/Kapra/Room-205/Gotham/Juniper/Maison faces -> logged open substitutes with matching width and cap height.
- Kept from donor: 115-to-11px type hierarchy, alternating full-bleed/cream rhythm, square and portrait media proportions, dense navigation, and compact footer labels.

## 7. Layout system

- Grid: loose asymmetric editorial sections alternating with strict two-up campaign grids.
- Section differentiation: full-bleed living media, small-object whitespace, paired social-style cards, arched rails, recognition bands, and an oversized footer wordmark.
- First screen: a full-bleed Ambika preparation/fruit film with the exact donor header and centred mark treatment; the primary CTA opens directions and the secondary CTA opens the menu.

## 9. Anti-goals

- No "inspired by Onyx" shortcut before the measured pixel clone.
- No generic smoothie startup gradients, floating blobs, or identical feature-card rows.
- No donor logos, copy, campaign marks, Arkansas facts, or coffee products in the shipping site.
- No invented prices, ingredients, health claims, awards, testimonials, or delivery promises.
- No decorative motion that weakens menu browsing or directions.

## 10. Deploy and backend expectation

- Deploy profile: Vercel preview after the asset gate; no production deploy.
- Backend: none. Static typed catalogue; no CMS, auth, database, checkout, or payments.

## 11. What must be true before this site is called ready

- The local clone reproduces Onyx across desktop, mobile, and reduced-motion evidence.
- The final Ambika site contains the full verified catalogue and working category/drink routes.
- `Get Directions` is the primary CTA and resolves to the verified shop location.
- Every donor sentence and asset is removed from shipping paths.
- Site checks and translation structure score pass, and the evidence packet is ready for human Beauty Pass review.
