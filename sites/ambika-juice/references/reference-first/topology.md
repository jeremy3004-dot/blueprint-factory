## Desktop Interaction Model

- Header is fixed/overlayed at the top and changes legibility treatment against light and dark media.
- Mega navigation opens by click/hover intent into a dense category panel; account, search, and cart are separate controls.
- The homepage conversion path is exploration-led: campaign CTA -> product/collection detail -> cart, with locations and support secondary.
- Media alternates between full-bleed autoplay video, static images, and inset cards; video is muted and loops.
- Product/origin rails use Swiper with touch/drag, arrow, and pagination behavior where present.
- Card media uses restrained hover zoom/opacity treatment; text links use underline/arrow movement rather than large button chrome.
- Footer categories and utility columns remain visible as a dense site map.

## Mobile Interaction Model

- The compact header preserves logo, menu, and cart/search controls; the mega-menu becomes a full-height drawer.
- Sections keep the desktop order but collapse splits to single columns, preserving generous cream whitespace between media blocks.
- Two-up cards stack, origin panels become horizontal touch rails, and editorial images alternate above/below their copy according to the captured rhythm.
- Display typography scales down sharply while letter spacing and all-caps hierarchy remain.
- Full-bleed video/image bands use portrait crops and maintain centred overlay copy.
- Footer groups remain dense but stack into narrow columns/accordion-like clusters without losing destinations.

## Typography Hierarchy

- Display/UI: Montserrat-style extended uppercase with aggressive tracking for campaign titles.
- Editorial/body: Bajern-like geometric sans with compact line lengths and small labels.
- Accent: script/logo artwork appears only as media/SVG, never as long-form copy.
- Captured type scale: 115 / 68 / 40 / 36 / 24 / 22 / 21 / 16 / 14 / 13 / 12 / 11px.
- Production translation must substitute proprietary fonts while preserving cap height, width, and tracking.

## Color And Spacing Rhythm

- Core palette: ink black `#000000`, warm paper `#fbfaf3`, white `#ffffff`, and secondary cream `#eee9df`.
- Long dark media bands alternate with quiet cream editorial sections; accents come from campaign assets rather than UI chrome.
- Desktop sections commonly run near viewport height or use 96-160px vertical breathing room; mobile sections retain 56-96px breathing room.
- Most cards are square or portrait with little/no radius; pill/circle radii are reserved for badges, carousel controls, and seals.

## Image And Video Strategy

- Hero and transition moments use full-bleed video or cinematic landscape imagery.
- Editorial sections pair large whitespace with one deliberately undersized square/portrait image.
- Campaign grids combine square social-style art, product still life, and documentary photography.
- Donor assets remain reference-only. Ambika ships only owned, licensed, or generated fruit, drink, preparation, staff, and shop imagery.
