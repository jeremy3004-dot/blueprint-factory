# Brief: Ambika Juice Pokhara

Status: approved

## What This Site Is

A full catalogue and brand website for Ambika Juice in Pokhara, built by first reproducing the Onyx Coffee Lab website locally at pixel-level fidelity and then translating the proven structure into Ambika's identity.

## Audience

- Pokhara residents looking for fresh juice, smoothies, lassi, milkshakes, fruit salads, and house specials.
- Domestic and international visitors searching for a trusted, affordable refreshment stop near Damside.
- Existing customers who want to see the menu, discover a drink, confirm hours, and get directions.

- Visual donor: `https://onyxcoffeelab.com`
