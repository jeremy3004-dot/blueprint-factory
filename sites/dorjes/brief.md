# Brief: Dorje's Resort & Spa

Dorje's is an eco-luxury boutique resort in Sedi Hills, Pokhara, overlooking Phewa Lake. The existing site is the sole source of truth for the property: 18 rooms and suites, locally rooted dining, pool and outdoor jacuzzi, spa and wellness, curated resort experiences, and the founder's community-minded story.

## Donor choice

Primary visual donor: **Aman** (`donor-aman`, https://www.aman.com). Its restrained editorial hierarchy, image-led storytelling, sparse typography, contained cinematic hero, asymmetric feature grid, and quiet conversion language fit Dorje's better than a conventional booking template.

## Derived scope

- `/` — homepage: brand, stay, dining, wellness, resort and founder story.
- `/accommodation-in-pokhara` — commercial rooms overview with all four real categories; live rate and availability remain in Dorje's booking system.
- `/tastes` — commercial dining overview using the resort's real farm-to-table, lake-bar and private-dining story.
- Booking remains on Dorje's existing booking system at `https://dorjes.com/booking/`; no new booking backend is invented.
- Wellness remains a major homepage pillar, but its older availability language is not promoted as a dedicated conversion page until the client confirms the current spa offer. Other current routes are represented in navigation where useful and deferred in `pages.json` with reasons.

## Audience and outcome

The primary audience is leisure travelers choosing a premium Pokhara stay: couples, families, wellness travelers and private-retreat guests. The site should feel calm, tactile and rare, make the lake setting immediately legible, and move visitors toward rooms or the existing booking flow without hype.
