Featured
Lauryn Prattes of Lauryn Prattes Events

When our clients work with Abby Jiu Phottography they get more than a photographer who just shows up on the wedding day to work for their 8-10 hour time frame.

They get someone who is interested in learning more about them, getting to know them on a personal level throughout the process, and communicating about the design and logistics leading up to the wedding day.
Laura Ritchie of Grit and Grace Inc.

Hiring Abby, Lisa, or Keila is the key to a successful event in our eyes!

Working with the ladies of Abby Jiu Photography is a planner's dream. We feel like an invincible power couple onsite when we get to work with them. AJP is always one step ahead of us with our wants and needs; they are attentive to our client's visions and know-how to get the shot without being overpowering the situation. We trust AJP with all of our best clients because we know they will always deliver an artful capture with something fashion-forward and real. Hiring Abby, Lisa, or Keila is the key to a successful event in our eyes!
Bride, Kayla Calloway

She was warm, thoughtful, and totally aligned with what we were looking for. Keila was truly my favorite hype woman — so encouraging and present. You can see her passion in the way she works; she's not just taking pictures, she's creating art.
Bride, Dani Mettel

Her dedication and attention to detail were clear in every step of the process. She even followed up quickly with our sneak peek images just after the weekend, which was such a fun surprise. So fast turning around images and very approachable and easy to work with. No ego at all."
